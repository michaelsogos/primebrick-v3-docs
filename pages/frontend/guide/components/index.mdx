Every component in `src/lib/components/` is listed here, grouped by origin.
**Custom** and **wrapped** components (we wrote or extended them) link to a
dedicated page on this site. **Vendored primitives** used unmodified link to
their official registry docs.

Provenance is recorded in
`docs/user-guide/_extracted/component-provenance.json` (hand-maintained). See
[UI stack](/docs/user-guide/ui-stack) for how the registries layer.
